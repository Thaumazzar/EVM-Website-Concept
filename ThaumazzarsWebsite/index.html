Welcome to ManenetDAO

Vision: ManeNetDAO aims for the improvement of the ecosystem, to coordinate qualified builders and to support other experienced community actors. We see a world with a cryptoeconomic landscape that is long-term, self-sufficient and regenerative. We pursue a greater symbiotic effect between the protocols and projects. We plant the seeds of tomorrows innovation. We facilitate high quality discussions about DeFi, NFTs and what has yet to be seen materialize upon the beacon chain and it's layers.

Values: We value decentralization of power, and activly supports a distributed Ethereum (and thorugh that a more fair world. ManeNetDAO is a project with long term approach to reaching our goals.

Public goods: ManeNetDAO emerges from the culture of open source software. ManeNetDAO treasury allocates funding towards public goods that honors this ethos, through avenues such as Gitcoin.

Education: The current technological landscape of Ethereum is a world of opportunities for ever more complex innovation. ManeNetDAO will host and distribute knowledge so the community can remain on the cutting edge on this new and exciting frontier.
